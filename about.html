Background Information


Vaaljapie Vintage is a sustainable and ethical vintage resell website that values second hand pieces. Buying vintage clothing is socially responsible, as each garment purchased combats the 14.3 million tons of textile waste per year produced in the United States alone. 84% of textile waste is sent to landfills at a cost of $3.7 billion per year, and a majority of this unprecedented growth in textile waste is driven by fast fashion and consumer behavior. The designer is Cadyn Fauber. She is currently a junior at West Virginia University. Her major is Graphic Design with a minor in Public Relations.
