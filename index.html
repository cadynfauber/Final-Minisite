Vaaljapie Impact

There’s mountains of trash out there. By choosing second hand appeal and decor, you help divert waste from landfills. Heres a snapshot at the difference we can make.











  • It takes up to 2,700 liters (713 gallons) of water to grow the cotton needed to make one t-shirt; a pair of jeans takes over 8,000 liters of water.




  • The fashion industry emits a hefty amount of greenhouse gases per year, thus contributing massively and actively to global warming.




  • Americans throw out anywhere from 60 to over 80 pounds of textile waste annually, and only about 10% of this makes it to thrift stores.
